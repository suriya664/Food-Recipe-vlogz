# Food Blog

A complete, responsive food blog website with recipes, cooking tips, and food stories.

## Features

- **Homepage** (2 variations) with hero section, latest posts, categories preview, and newsletter signup
- **Recipes Page** with grid layout, filters (cuisine, difficulty, time, diet), and search functionality
- **Single Recipe Page** with ingredients, step-by-step instructions, prep/cook time, nutrition facts, and comments
- **About Page** with story, team information, and professional photos
- **Blog Posts Page** for non-recipe content (food stories, tips, tutorials, travel guides, kitchen hacks)
- **Categories Page** with all food categories (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Desserts, Drinks, Vegetarian/Vegan, etc.)
- **Contact Page** with contact form, email, and social media links
- **Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, FAQ, and 404 Error pages**
- **Shop Page** (optional) for e-books, recipe books, cooking classes, and merchandise
- **Newsletter/Subscribe Page** with subscription form and lead magnet

## Design Features

- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Consistent navbar with active page highlighting
- Clickable logo linking to homepage
- Favicon on all pages
- Professional footer with social media links
- Hover effects on all buttons and links
- Form validation with success/error messages
- Smooth scrolling and transitions

## Getting Started

2. All pages are linked and ready to use
3. Add your favicon to the `assets/` folder
